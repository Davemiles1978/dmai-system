# DMAI Quick Reference

## Wake Words
"Hey DMAI" - Wakes her up

## Basic Commands
- "What's my status?" - System check
- "Create a video about [topic]" - Generate content
- "Research [topic]" - Find information
- "Send to my phone/laptop" - Deliver results
- "Where's my iPhone?" - Locate device
- "Call me [David/Master/Father/Sir]" - Change name

## Name Rules
ONLY: David, Master, Father, Sir
NEVER: Dave, Buddy, etc.

## Devices Connected
- MacBook (always)
- iPhone (registered)

## After 20:00
1. Enroll voice: `python voice/enroll_master.py`
2. Service runs automatically: `./dmai_service.sh status`
3. Just say "Hey DMAI" anytime
